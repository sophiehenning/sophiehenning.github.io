# About me
Hi, I am a PhD student in [Alexander Fraser's](https://alexfraser.github.io/) group at the Technical University of Munich in Heilbronn, working on uncertainty in deep-learning based Natural Language Processing. 
My PhD journey started in 2021 at the [Bosch Center for Artificial Intelligence (BCAI)](https://www.bosch-ai.com/) under the supervision of [Annemarie Friedrich](https://annefried.github.io) (previously Bosch, now University of Augsburg) and Alexander Fraser (LMU Munich back then). From January 2024 till mid-2026, I worked full-time as a Senior Data Scientist for AI Enabling at BCAI, teaching NLP and Machine Learning in an enterprise context, before returning to academia in July 2026.

You can find an up-to-date list of my publications [here](https://dblp.org/pid/222/3050.html).
